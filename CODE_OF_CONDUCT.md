# Code of Conduct

Issue-Ink is an open, collaborative experiment.

We are committed to providing a respectful and inclusive environment.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Accept that others may overwrite your pixels.
- Engage in creative collaboration.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory symbols
- Explicit or illegal content
- Automated abuse or spam

The maintainers reserve the right to remove content or restrict access if necessary.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an issue labeled "moderation".

All reports will be reviewed fairly.
